mayameme is a researcher, writer, and activist who has been working at the intersection of media, technology, human rights, and feminism. She has been working at Tactical Technology Collective since early 2010. She is also a doctoral candidate at Leuphana University, Luneburg, focusing on how ethics is being framed in the context of driverless cars. She has Masters degrees from the University of Sussex in Media and Cultural Studies, and from Delhi University (Applied Psychology).
