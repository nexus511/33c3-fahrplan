Speaker: Lisa Tschorn

Geographer and artist.

Lisa Tschorn is a human being. While studying a master degree in human geography, it became more and more clear, that the problems we face today will not be solved be the minds that created them. After finishing her master she went to art school.
She also published a book:
Shadia Husseini de Araújo, Tobias Schmitt, Lisa Tschorn (Ed.) Widerständigkeiten im ›Land der Zukunft‹ Andere Blicke auf und aus Brasilien. Münster
Currently I am working on this video project: "Ein Ort. Eine Frage".
