Speaker: Max Mehl

Max Mehl is Germany Coordinator of the Free Software Foundation Europe (FSFE). His working field is German and European policy and public awareness for Free Software and user rights.

In recent years he was active around the Compulsory Routers case in Germany, EU Radio Lockdown Directive, „I love Free Software“ day, DRM, or FSFE’s „Free Your Android“ campaign.
