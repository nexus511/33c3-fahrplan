Speaker: Julia Reda

Member of the European Parliament
European Pirate Party / Greens/EFA group

Julia Reda is a member of the European Parliament for the European Pirate Party and a Vice Chair of the Greens/EFA group. She advocates for the free exchange of culture and knowledge across borders and for progress that benefits all.

Julia was listed among the „30 under 30 championing Europeans’ rights“ by Forbes and named the second-most influential MEP for internet policy by netzpolitik.org.
