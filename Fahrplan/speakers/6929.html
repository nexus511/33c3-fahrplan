Speaker: Roland Schilling

Roland Schilling holds a Dipl.-Ing. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Hamburg University of Technology (TUHH) and has been working as a Ph.D. Student and Research Assistant in the institute of Security in Distributed
Applications at TUHH for almost five years with a research focus on mobile communication security. During this time he has been teaching classes in software- and application security, as well as introductory security lectures.
